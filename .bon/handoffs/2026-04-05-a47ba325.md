## For Claudes to come

When bons are well-drawn — clear `--what` with numbered steps, `--how` with approach constraints, `--done` with verifiable criteria — they replace plan files. This session executed two actions plus an unlisted MCP piece by busking (no plan file, no plan mode) because the bons contained enough architectural intent to maintain flow state. The test: after creating a bon, could you delete the plan file with no information loss? If yes, you planned correctly. If the bon is terse and the plan has the detail, the information is in the wrong place.

Starlette's `Route()` without `methods` parameter is GET-only, not all-methods. This is undocumented and counterintuitive. Always pass `methods=["GET", "POST"]` explicitly. We burned 10 minutes on a 405 before discovering this.
